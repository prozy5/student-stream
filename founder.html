Founder Story

Deondre founded StudentStream with a simple belief — creators should own their platforms, their content, and their future.

What began as a streaming concept evolved into a multi-division technology company connecting media, production, law, and investigative storytelling under one ecosystem.

Instead of waiting for opportunity, Deondre built the infrastructure from the ground up.

Letter from the Founder

StudentStream was never meant to be just another platform.

It was built because creators deserve more than broken systems, unfair contracts, and platforms that profit from their work while offering little in return.

SS Films is the first step — a place where independent creators can publish original stories, build real audiences, and earn without giving up ownership.

Every division under StudentStream exists for the same reason: to build systems that creators control, not corporations.

This is only the beginning.

— Deondre
