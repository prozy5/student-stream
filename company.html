StudentStream is a technology company focused on building modern streaming, monetization, and creator-first systems designed for independent creators and digital media.

Our Mission

Our mission is to give creators ownership, freedom, and real opportunities to grow — without relying on outdated platforms, unfair algorithms, or broken monetization systems.

We believe creators should control their content, their audience, and how they earn. StudentStream is built to provide the infrastructure that makes that possible.

Founder

Founder & CEO

Deondre

Deondre is the owner and founder of the technology company StudentStream and the creator behind its first division, SS Films.

He started StudentStream with the goal of building platforms that give creators real control over their content, revenue, and audience — without depending on traditional media companies or unfair systems.

What began as an idea to support independent creators has grown into a full platform focused on streaming, creator tools, and monetization infrastructure designed for the next generation of digital media.

“Creators deserve ownership, freedom, and systems that actually work for them. StudentStream is built to make that possible.”

Our Vision

StudentStream aims to become a full ecosystem for creators — combining streaming, monetization, analytics, advertising systems, and creator services into one unified platform.

We are building long-term infrastructure for digital creators to operate independently, scale sustainably, and build real businesses around their work.
